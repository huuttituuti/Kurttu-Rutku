written by a small pigeon

Kurttu is a Finnish pigeon living in Finland. Since he was little, Kurttu has always dreamt of becoming a writer. "But why would a pigeon ever want that?", you may ask. Well, he's so full of sarcasm he need's an outlet or he'll burst into atoms. It's a common medical condition affecting small grumpy pigeons.

So far Kurttu has written two guides, one to Finland and one to the oldest city in Finland, Turku. These guides are written with tongue in cheek and are a guide and a comic book crossbreed that everyone with a high-quality sense of humor will surely enjoy!
