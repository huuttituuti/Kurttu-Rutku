Last updated: 22.07.2024

We use cookies on our website. By clicking on the "Accept all cookies" button, you agree to our use of cookies. Cookies make using our site easy, fast and user-friendly. Cookies also enable us to develop our business.

1. What are cookies?

A cookie is a small text file that an internet browser stores on a user's device. Cookies are only placed on the user's terminal device when the user visits a website. Only the server that sent the cookie can later read and use the cookie. Cookies or other technologies do not harm the user's terminal equipment or files, nor can cookies be used to run programs or spread malware. Cookies alone do not allow the user to be identified.

Cookies are divided into subcategories: mandatory cookies, functional cookies, product development and business reporting, advertising reporting and advertising targeting. Mandatory cookies cannot be blocked. They are related to security and enabling the site to function. Mandatory cookies do not contain personally identifiable information.

2. What cookies do we use?

Necessary cookies

We use essential cookies to enable the functionality of our website that is necessary for your use.

On the Products pages, we use the browser's own Session storage. This is to remember whether the user has chosen to close or keep open the Filters element. When a user has a tab open on the Products page, but for example reloads the page, the user's previous choice to open or close the elements is remembered.However, when a user ends a session, i.e. the user leaves the site or deletes a tab on the Products page, Session storage record is also deleted.

Analytics and marketing

Analytical cookies for service development allow us to track visits and traffic sources in order to measure and improve the performance of our site. This allows us to improve our service for the benefit of our customers.

Marketing cookies allow us to obtain information about our customers' activity on our site. These allow us to measure and improve the effectiveness of our advertising.

We use Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, Google Ads and Google Adsense to do this. You can read the Google Privacy Statement at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

These cookies do not contain personally identifiable information and we ask your permission to place statistical cookies.
