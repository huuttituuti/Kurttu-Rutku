Experience it rationally!

Have you ever strolled around in a foreign city and at some point noticed that when you tried to spot the city's main attractions and that famously weird Little pissing man statue, you ended up walking back and forth, and that step counter app you're using starts to show alarmingly high numbers?

To avoid overtraining, we have kindly taken this feature into account in our Guide to Turku. Thanks to our pioneering and exceptional technology, the sights of Turku are arranged logically so that you don't have to spend your precious time walking in circles!

Your guide to Turku

Want to experience Finland's oldest city from the comfort of your couch? And even more importantly, do you want to look at nice pictures and read funny comics?

In the Guide to Turku, Kurttu writes about Turku's most famous attractions and his own experiences there. Kurttu also has, for some reason, gotten an artist to draw these experiences into one-page cartoons.

Still unsure whether you'd like it or not? Get a little sneak peek by pressing the preview button below! You can read two pages of the publication in question for free (the physical copy is, of course, without the watermarks, and with a much better image quality).
